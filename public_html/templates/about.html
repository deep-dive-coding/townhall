About us

The Townhall story begins in Albuquerque NM, 2017 at CNM's Deep Dive Coding Bootcamp. Ryan, Steven, Leonora and Michelle were assigned to build a web application as a team. They considered the importance of ways to improve the City we all call home. Over the next few weeks, Albuquerque Virtual Townhall was born.

Mission

Our mission is to give the people of Albuquerque the power to create and share ideas and concerns instantly and communicate with their elected officals without barriers.

Values

Diversity: We know it takes people with different ideas, strengths, interests, and cultural backgrounds for Townhall to succeed. Respect: We strive to show a deep respect for all people and for the communities in which they live. Collaboration: We believe that if weleverage collective genius, together we make the difference. Social value: We encourage healthy debate and differences of opinion. Democracy: We think it is important to have a forum for a civil exchange of ideas on the internet.
